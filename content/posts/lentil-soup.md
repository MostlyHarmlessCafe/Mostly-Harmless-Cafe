Lentil soup isn't exactly culinary greatness, but it is incredibly hearty, easy to cook, easy to keep, and cost effective.

# Ingredients
- Extra virgin olive oil
- 1 large red onion (diced)
- salt and pepper
- 200g of carrots (peeled, finely grated/diced)
- 100g of celery (thinly sliced)
- 400g of baby bella mushrooms (rinsed and sliced &frac14;-inch thick)
- 4 cloves of garlic (minced)
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp of dried oregano
- 1&frac12; tsp of dried basil
- &frac12; tsp of ground cayenne pepper
- 15 oz. can of crushed tomatoes
- 1 lb (&approx;450g) of dried navy lentils
- water
- 40g fresh parsley (rinsed and chopped)
- red wine vinegar
- *Optional: Hot sauce*

# Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 300 &deg;F.
1. To a large pot on medium-low heat, add the olive oil and onions with a pinch of salt.
1. Gently cook until onions are caramelized/browned.
1. Add the carrots and celery. Stir in. Cook until the carrots and celery have softened.
1. Add the mushrooms with about &frac12; cup of water. Stir in. Gently cook until the mushrooms have softened slightly.
1. Add the garlic. Stir in. Cook until fragrant.
1. Add the bay leaf, oregano, basil, and cayenne pepper.
1. Add the crushed tomatoes. Stir in.
1. Add the parsley and stir in.
1. Add the lentils and stir in.
1. Add water to cover the lentils.
1. Bring to a simmer and put in the oven, covered. Cook for &approx;40 minutes.
1. When you pull it out of the oven, it will likely look a bit dry. Add water until it reaches your preferred consistency.
1. Add to taste red wine vinegar.
1. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
1. Serve by itself, on rice, with bread, or whatever you want.
